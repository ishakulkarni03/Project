About Us

Ngo-needs is a platform that allows users to donate commodities based on the needs of respective NGOs. Donating to such NGOs ensures that resources are used effectively and go directly to those in need. A donation portal is a platform that allows users to donate commodities based on the needs of respective NGOs. Previously, we were required to visit each NGO and ask them about their requirements, but now this problem can be easily solved by visiting a website that has a collection of NGOs and their requirements. Also, this is a huge platform for donors as they have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on various causes, ranging from education and healthcare to environmental conservation and disaster relief.
